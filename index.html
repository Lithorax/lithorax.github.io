01

Geological Toolbox

Nine free, in-browser calculators covering sedimentology, geochemistry, hydrogeology, stratigraphy, and planetary science. No login. No download. Just science.

🪨
Sedimentology

Grain Size Classifier

Classify sediment by Wentworth Scale. Input mm, μm, or Φ units. Returns depositional environment interpretation.

→
⚗️
Geochemistry

CIA Calculator

Chemical Index of Alteration from major oxide inputs. Quantifies intensity of silicate weathering.

→
📊
Geochemistry

Geochemical Indices

Compute CIA, PIA, CIW, ICV, and WIP simultaneously from a single oxide dataset.

→
💧
Hydrogeology

Darcy's Law

Solve for flow rate Q, hydraulic conductivity K, or gradient i from the classic groundwater equation.

→
🔵
Petrophysics

Porosity Calculator

Calculate porosity from density or volume measurements. Optional Kozeny-Carman permeability estimate.

→
Φ
Sedimentology

Phi Scale Converter

Bidirectional conversion between millimetres and the phi (Φ) scale. Includes Wentworth reference table.

→
⚖️
Petrophysics

Rock Density Estimator

Bulk density from mass/volume or from mineral volume fractions. Returns rock-type inference.

→
⏳
Stratigraphy

Geologic Time Scale

Interactive reference for the International Chronostratigraphic Chart — filterable by eon and searchable by period.

→
⭕
Planetary Geology

Impact Crater Calculator

Estimate final crater diameter using Pi-scaling relations (Schmidt & Housen 1987). Supports Earth, Mars, Moon, Venus.
